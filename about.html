About Dhrti

Connecting Communities and Recyclers

Dhrti is a pioneering platform that bridges the gap between local plastic waste suppliers and industrial recyclers, creating a seamless, rewarding recycling experience. By enabling individuals and vendors to submit plastic waste, estimate their earnings, schedule pickups, and track their impact, Dhrti empowers communities to actively engage in sustainable waste management.
