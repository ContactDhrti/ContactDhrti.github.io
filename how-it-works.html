How Dhrti Works

Dhrti's process is simple, efficient, and impactful. Here's how we connect communities and industries to transform plastic waste into value.

01

Collect

Local vendors and individuals gather plastic waste from their communities and bring it to Dhrti's collection points. Through our platform, they can easily schedule pickups or find drop-off locations, making it convenient and accessible.

Easy Scheduling Convenient Locations Flexible Options
02

Earn

For every kilogram of plastic collected, vendors earn instant cash through Dhrti's "Instant Earnings Calculator," empowering them with additional income while actively contributing to environmental cleanup.

Instant Payments Fair Rates Transparent System
03

Connect

Dhrti links local vendors and communities with businesses and industrial partners that can recycle or repurpose plastic waste. Our platform ensures that collected plastic reaches the right recycling or repurposing facilities.

Direct Connections Quality Assurance Verified Partners
04

Facilitate Exchange

Dhrti provides a transparent marketplace for connecting processed plastic materials with businesses and industrial buyers in need of recycled resources. This fosters a circular economy where waste materials are reused.

Secure Transactions Market Rates Efficient Process
05

Measure Impact

Our platform provides metrics to track the positive environmental contributions made by each participant, inspiring a culture of sustainability and accountability within communities and industries.

Real-time Tracking Impact Reports Environmental Metrics
