Our Mission

At Dhrti, we are committed to transforming the way plastic waste is managed by creating a sustainable ecosystem that bridges local communities with industries that recycle and repurpose plastic waste. Our mission is to foster a cleaner, greener planet by making recycling accessible, transparent, and rewarding for everyone involved.

Empowering Communities

Dhrti empowers local people and vendors to actively participate in recycling efforts by offering fair incentives and simplifying the process of collecting and exchanging plastic waste. We aim to create economic opportunities while fostering environmental responsibility at a grassroots level.

Building a Circular Economy

By connecting suppliers with industries that use recycled plastic, Dhrti promotes a circular economy. We ensure that plastic waste gets a second life, reducing the need for virgin plastics and minimizing environmental impact.

Driving Innovation in Recycling

Through partnerships with forward-thinking industries and organizations, Dhrti drives innovation in recycling and waste management, opening doors to new technologies and sustainable practices.

Educating and Inspiring Change

Dhrti believes in the power of knowledge. Our platform provides resources and educational content to raise awareness about the benefits of recycling and responsible waste management, inspiring people to join the movement for a cleaner future.

At Dhrti, we envision a world where plastic waste is not a problem, but a valuable resource. Together, we can reduce plastic pollution, support communities, and create a lasting positive impact on our planet.
